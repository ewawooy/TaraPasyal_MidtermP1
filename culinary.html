Strawberry Taho

This shot was taken when I went to Burnham Park in Baguio last July 2022.

Personally, I typically avoid eating strawberries, but when I tried this strawberry taho, I was pleasantly surprised to find that I enjoyed it. The sweetness was familiar, yet what intrigued me most was the delightful kick of strawberry flavor that came through.

I wish that I can try this again.

Oyster

This shot was taken when we visited our province Iloilo last 2021.

Oysters, known locally as 'Talaba,' are highly popular in Iloilo. I remember trying them for the first time; initially, the salty taste was a bit uncomfortable. However, when my mother suggested adding vinegar and chili, it surprisingly tasted delicious.

Chicken

This photo was taken during our trip to Taiwan in November 2024.

We tried Taiwanese chicken, and it was definitely different from what I’m used to. Instead of being fried or packed with seasoning, it seemed to be simply boiled or steamed. The texture was really tender, and the flavor was light but natural—it felt like the focus was on the quality of the chicken itself.
