Shihfen

This photo was taken during my trip to Taiwan in November 2024. I was at Shifen, a place that felt like it came straight out of a movie—nostalgic, lively, and full of charm.

Walking along the railway tracks, I was surrounded by little shops selling all sorts of souvenirs and mouthwatering street food. But the real magic? Watching people release sky lanterns, each one carrying hopes and dreams into the night sky. It was one of those moments that made me pause, take it all in, and feel grateful to be there.
