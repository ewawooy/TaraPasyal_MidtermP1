About Me

Hey there stranger, welcome! My name is Gilliana Meekaela J. Mina and I am the owner of this travel blog. I'm happy and thrilled to have you here as I share my experiences. Trying new things such as tasting new dishes and visiting different places, has always made me happy. Through this blog, I aim to share my adventures and stories with fellow travelers like you.

You may be wondering why I started this travel blog. The reason is simple: I believe that beautiful things deserve to be seen and discovered all around the world. Each dish, destination, and trip that you'll see here has its own unique story. I also hope to inspire people to step out of their comfort zones and understand that it's okay to let yourself experience something new.

Through this blog, I'll be able to share photos of various dishes and places I have experienced. Each photo is a snapshot of a moment that tells a story and invites you to immerse yourself in it.

Thank you for being here and visiting my blog! I hope you enjoy! Let's discover the world's wonders together.
